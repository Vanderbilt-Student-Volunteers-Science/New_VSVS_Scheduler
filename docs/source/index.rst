Welcome to VSVS_Scheduler's documentation!
==========================================
This project was developed to assist Vanderbilt Student Volunteers for Science (VSVS), a student organization at
Vanderbilt University, with matching volunteers into teams of 3-4 people that can visit one of our partner schools at
the same time. To learn more about our organization visit: https://studentorg.vanderbilt.edu/vsvs/
